## Overview  

**Providers** are the carriers and gateways that move your voice and messaging traffic outside Unpod.  
You can use **Unpod’s built-in SIP trunking** for simplicity or connect your own telecom partners for flexibility and cost control.  

In this guide, you’ll learn how to:  
1. Add a SIP provider  
2. Configure credentials and routing  
3. Connect providers to bridges  
---

## Prerequisites  

- A verified **Unpod account**  
- At least **one virtual number or SIP trunk**  
- Credentials for your chosen SIP provider (Vapi, LiveKit, Daily)  
---

## Add a New Provider  

1. Open the **Bridge** where you want to use this provider  
2.Open **Providers** → **➕ Add Provider**  
3. Select your provider:  
   - **Vapi**  
   - **LiveKit**  
   - **Daily**  
4. Enter the required credentials:  
   - **SIP URI or WebSocket URL**  
   - **Username / Password** (if applicable)  
5. Click **Save**  

> _All calls from numbers linked to this bridge will now route through the selected provider._
