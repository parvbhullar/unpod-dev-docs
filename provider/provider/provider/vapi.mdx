### Configure Number with Vapi

1. After adding a number to a Bridge, click **Configure**.  

<img src="./images/TelephonyVapiStep1.png" alt="Telephony Vapi Step 1" />

2. Select **Vapi** from the provider list.  

<img src="./images/TelephonyVapiStep2.png" alt="Telephony Vapi Step 2" />

3. Click **Configure** next to Vapi.  

<img src="./images/TelephonyVapiStep3.png" alt="Telephony Vapi Step 3" />

<img src="./images/TelephonyVapiStep7.png" alt="Telephony Vapi Step 7" />
