## Getting Started

**Unpod: An open source platform for building Voice Agents**

Unpod lets you create AI voice identities with unique phone numbers that handle calls and messages, filter unnecessary communications, and provide real-time insights. Connect with your existing communication channels, data sources, and tools — and let your AI identity streamline conversations and execute actions across them.

## Key Capabilities

- **AI Voice Identity** — Create personalized AI agents with unique phone numbers
- **Multi-Channel Support** — Voice, WhatsApp, and Email from a single platform
- **Smart Filtering** — Reduce noise and surface what matters
- **Real-Time Insights** — Summaries and context tracking across conversations
- **Workflow Automation** — Execute tasks like scheduling and CRM updates from conversations
- **Integration Ecosystem** — Connect to CRMs, ERPs, data sources, and existing tools
- **Flexible Routing** — Use numbers, providers, and bridges to control traffic
